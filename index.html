About Us

VSWS was founded in the loving memory of Shri Vijay Shanker Tare, a selfless social worker who devoted his life to serving others. Under the leadership of Smt. Tilottama Tare, the Society works tirelessly to support marginalized children and women, helping them access education, health services, and the skills to build better futures.


We believe that education is the most powerful tool for social change. At VSWS, we strive to end child labor and promote literacy by bringing children into classrooms, not landfills. Our goal is to raise responsible, empowered individuals who lead their communities with integrity and compassion.


Our vision is to create a society where childhood is joyful, womanhood is empowered, and the environment is respected. Our mission is to drive awareness and action around social and ecological issues, enabling the deprived to realize their full potential through education, care, and community support.

What we Do

Education for Underprivileged Children

We believe that no child should be denied the chance to learn. Our team identifies children forced into child labour — including rag picking — and helps transition them into schools. We provide academic support, life skills training, and moral education to ensure they not only attend school but thrive in it.

Women’s Empowerment

We offer skill development programs, vocational training, and awareness sessions to equip women with the tools to become financially independent. Our focus is on building confidence, resilience, and leadership from the ground up.

Adult Literacy and Life Skills

Our adult literacy initiatives teach reading, writing, spoken English, and essential knowledge around health, hygiene, and etiquette. These programs help individuals build confidence and self-worth, and open doors to better employment opportunities.

Environmental Action

We actively promote sustainable, eco-friendly practices. From awareness drives to local clean-up activities, we strive to instill environmental responsibility in the communities we serve — because a better world must also be a greener one.
